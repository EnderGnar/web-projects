Conway's Game of Life

Wikipedia

In this implementation you can draw your own starting scenarios. The pen can be changed to any number of pen by pressing the up and down buttons. This will also change your preview.

To run this simulation you can either press on Simulate repeatedly or select Autospeed and then press simulate once.

To reuse a creature you press on Select, if it turns green you mark the area and then press on copy. Now it can be found under your pens.
