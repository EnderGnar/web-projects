Welcome to 3d Planets

This project was built as a prototype of how we could use our own 3d engine to do more than just Rubiks cube animations.

At that time we did not have a clear idea on how to effectively create perspective, which is why the field of view is very limited.

Navigate using WASD to move in the XY plane and Space and Shift to move up or down. Dragging the mouse turns the camera.

Use N to turn all planets into circles.
